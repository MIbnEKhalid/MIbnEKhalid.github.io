What Web Tools Do You Use for Website hosting, Business Email, etc?
I currently use a combination of tools and platforms tailored to my needs:

Website Hosting

  • Main Website (`mbktechstudio.com`): Hosted on GitHub Pages.
  • Subdomains: Hosted on Netlify and Vercel, with each subdomain having its own GitHub repository serving as a "bucket" for its respective site.
  • All websites are frontend-only, meaning no backend servers are required.

Domain Registration

  • I registered my domain with Namecheap.

Business Email

  • I use Zoho Mail's free plan, which provides up to 5 business email accounts.

Form Handling

  • I use Web3Forms to efficiently handle contact form submissions and other types of form data without needing a backend service.

Cost

Most of the tools I use are free or have a free tier that meets my needs. My total recurring cost for domain hosting is $14–16 annually.
